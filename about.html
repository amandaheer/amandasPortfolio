Teaching Career

I have worked as an elementary school teacher for the past 8 years. I enjoy working with children and making their educational experience engaging and lively. I did an Integrational Arts certificate through BYU which allowed me to create curriculum that integrated visual art, dance, music, and drama. My students absolutley loved learning their core subjects through the arts. It was Covid that made me realize that education was changing in a way that I didn't love.

My Journey Into UX/UI Design

I had a friend introduce me to TechMoms. I was hesitant to try this out as it was far from anything education related. During my TechMom's course, my eyes were opened to different careers that women were doing in tech. Each week we had a different female speaker come to class and do a presentation on thier career feild such as UX/UI, Front-end coder, Data Analysis, and Cyber Security. We also learned basic coding such as HTML, CSS, and some JavaScript.

Future Goals
