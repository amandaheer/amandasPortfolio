Introduction

Our team decided to create an app that would help people be able to find a rated doctor and book an appointment with that doctor. This will help users meet thier specific health needs in one organized location. As UX Designers, we planned on conducting an interview plan and using the data we collect to create a final digital prototype that is user friendly.

User Research

Over the next three weeks our team conducted 3-5 interviews in order to define our user and the problem at hand. We worked together to lay out our findings and list needs to which our product will meet. Once we had a good indication of specific features, we began to build our prototype. From the data we gathered, we were able to organize it into our affinity diagarm and empathy map. This information helped us conclude our research with the creation of our user persona.

Definition & Ideation

Our problem statment concludes as...MyDoctor was designed to achieve a simplified way for patients to find the correct medical specialist for their needs. We have observed that users struggle to confidently select a healthcare provider while sorting through a myriad of options. How might MyDoctor streamline the search for healthcare so that customers are satisfied with their selection? We created a value proposition of "The intuitive way to find the specialized care you need."

Prototyping

We completed a competitor anaylsis with other similar apps to MyDoctor such as "ZOCDOC" and "WEBMD". This information helped us form the beginning stages of designing our app with some early sketches. Once the sketches were complete, we did another several user tests to test out the over design. There were many iterations that we went forward with changing from the user tests.

Testing & Iterating

Now that our changes were completed on our sketches, we went forward with creating the iOS mockups and final prototype on Figma.

Final Thoughts

Our team really enjoyed working on this project together and watching it unfold. Our idea began as a doctor app that was able to find people a specific doctor in their native language. That grew into providing many filter options such as insurance, male/female doctor, picture, bio, and more, so that our users could narrow down their search easily to find their perfect doctor. It was amazing how much faster our team could get the research, interviews, and project completed when working on it together. It was also insightful to be able to share ideas with each other and brainstorm when problem solving. If we were to launch this app,, we would want to improve it by creating a booking function. This would allow our users to go one step further past saving their prefered doctor by being able to book a time and day to meet with the doctor all through the MyDoctor app. It would save a huge step or our users and be faster than calling a doctors office to try and schedule an appointment. Our team is happy with the result of our app and we were grateful to have had each other to help build our UX design skills.
